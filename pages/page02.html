White Tea



Made from Cameilia sinensis tea plant. This one is slightly oxidized for production to make white tea. Some popular varieties include, Silver Needle (Baâ€¦ Yinzhert) and White Peony (Bai Mudan). These are made from immature buds of the tea plant that are picked and processed before they have time to ripen and develop many characteristics, that are normally associated with tea. Due to the underdevelopment, they tend to have much less caffeine than any other tea varieties. This tea produces a pale liquor color and are the most subtle of teas. Some examples of white teas is the, White Ambrosia tea, White Calypso tea, and White Bamboo Tea. The white tea is primarily produced in China.
